TTS Form

TTS Form is an accessibility-focused application aimed at helping people in low-literacy areas fill out forms using voice input. It captures and transcribes audio using speech recognition and auto-fills fields. It also allows users to translate form labels into local languages like Gujarati — helping those who can't read understand the form. The project makes digital participation more inclusive.

  • Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SpeechRecognition, gTTS, Accessibility, VoiceToText
